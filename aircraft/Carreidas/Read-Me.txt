A little history
================

After nearly 5 years without a new album, Herge and Tintin is in a fantastic adventure footprint "Vol 714 pour Sydney." On this occasion Roger Leloup 
(author of the comic Yoko Tsuno) draws a remarkable aircraft in 1966. The Carreidas 160 Jet. Capable of flying at Mach 2, variable geometry wing etc ... 
A vision of the future impressive today. 

Not having accurate technical data (it seems that technical data is published in the Journal of Tintin "by December 1966) I tried to recreate 
this device as fairly as possible. You will pardon me the possible errors. Obviously any information trying to improve this model will be welcome.
