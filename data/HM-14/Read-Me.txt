A little history
================

1933 First flight of the HM 14.

1934, publication of the book of Henry MIGNET "Le Sport de l'air" and presentation of the HM-14 to the Air Show.
Henry MIGNET, thanks to his book, will cause a number of vocations of aviators amateurs. Indeed, in its work it 
explains simply, but methodically, how to build is even its own plane. In fact, the more known HM 14 under the name of:

POU DU CIEL

Until his death on August 31, 1965, Henry MIGNET did not cease improving his formula of plane. It is certain that without 
him aviation amateur would not be what it is. In the honor of its memory here thus, for you, the HM 14 of the origins.
