Readme File for Pilatus PC-21 v2.0 (development)

Flight data model and systems by Ernest Teuscher
Models, instruments and liveries by Petar Jedvaj

Thank you for downloading the Pilatus PC-21 package.

This package is released under GPLv3 license.

The Pilatus PC-21 is a single-turboprop, low wing swept monoplane advanced trainer with a stepped tandem cockpit manufactured by Pilatus Aircraft of Switzerland.

Thanks to Johan G. for research and great livery, Alan Kenwright for testing and fixes and Rob Dosogne for the instruments.

Big thanks to David Culp and Erik Hofman, this PC-21 package is based on their PC-7 package.
